Broom Broom is a video game like chrome dino but you don't need to turn off the internet to play. In this game the player controlls a magical broom that was stolen from a which by her cat. Now, Meowrina the cat travels through the forest at an ever increasing speed. As Meorina can't handle the broom well, it's your task to help her get past the ghouls and creatures of the forest safely.

The aim of the video game is to avoid all the obstacles towards the player. As time progresses, the broom starts flying at a greater speed and appear faster, thus increasing the difficulty in avoiding them. The more the player can manage to not get caught by a creature the more points they collect and can build a highscore. The game is over when a ghoul or other evil creature collides with the broom. Then, the player can startg over.

Boom Broom was made with Unity.

This game was made solely with free assets from various artist that can be found onlne.
